Cryptid Fun was created in 2023 by Hannah Hurst to help people learn more about the amazing monsters that exist on the edges of our cutlural experiences.
From stories passed down through generations, to urban myths created by people on the internet. Cryptids are a part of our world, no matter if they are real or not.
Our aim is to get people to love these creatures just as much as we do. We hope you enjoy our site!
